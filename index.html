Cat

Cat is a domestic animal. They are very beautiful and friendly animals. Their bodies are covered with smooth fur. They sleep a lot during the day and eat both animal and vegetables. They belong to the same family of tigers and panthers.Cats are of three kinds, namely- farm cats, domestic cats and feral cats. House cats are petted by many people all across the globe. The average life spam of a domestic cat is between 15 to 18 years. A cat offspring is called a kitten. Cats are very protective and caring towards their kittens. They feed the kittens and raise them. Kittens are extremely tiny and adorable as well.
